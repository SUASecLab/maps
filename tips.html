Tips on using the SUASecLab:

You can move around using WASD keys.

You can use the Shift key to move faster.

Please use a headset if possible.

Please use the latest version of your browser.

Please use a device with a hardware keyboard.

Please enable JavaScript for this site.

Please make sure your privacy settings allow accessing your camera and microphone

Group conversations can grow up to 4 participants. Use the Jitsi lounges for bigger conferences.

Press Space when slide-in window messages appear or click on them to open the slide-in window.

When slide-in windows are opened, you can close them by clicking on the 'x' button on the gray bar. You can resize the slide-in window by dragging the gray bar.

If you experience issues with the slide-in windows, retry to open them by moving out of the special zone and entering it again. If that does not work, reload the website.

You can customize your character and change the game settings by clicking on the upper left button.

If you want to report a bug or reach out to the development team, please contact Tobias Tefke <t.tefke@stud.fh-sm.de>

You can always find these tips in the menu (using the coffee cup in the upper left corner).
